Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, and the expense records you enter. We do not collect any payment information or sensitive financial account details.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Spendly service. This includes displaying your expense history, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. However, no method of storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services for hosting or analytics. These services have their own privacy policies and may receive limited technical data (such as IP addresses or browser types) as part of normal web operations. We do not share your personal expense data with any third party.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly.
